When a broker submits custom policy wording for a client, an underwriter must assess whether the proposed
wording departs from the insurer&#39;s standard position, introduces unacceptable legal exposure, or conflicts with firm
red-lines. This review is currently manual, slow, and inconsistently applied across the team.

## Objective
Design an AI-assisted review system that helps an underwriter answer:
1. What wording differs materially from the insurer&#39;s standard position?
2. What additional legal or coverage risk does that difference create?
3. What exact text or clause caused each finding?
4. Has a similar non-standard position been approved before — and how relevant is that precedent?
